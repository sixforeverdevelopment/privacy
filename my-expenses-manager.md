# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 15, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use My Expenses Manager ("the App"). We believe in your right to privacy, especially concerning your personal and financial information. 

Our philosophy is simple: **Your data belongs to you, and it stays on your device.**

## Data Collection and Usage

**We do not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal or financial data on our servers.**

All information you enter into the App—including your income, expenses, categories, and tags—is stored **locally** on your device using native, on-device storage mechanisms. 

Because we do not collect your data:
- We do not have access to your financial records.
- We cannot share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties.
- We cannot recover your data if you lose your device or uninstall the App without a backup.

## Analytics and Tracking

The App does not include any third-party tracking software, analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase), or ad networks that silently collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage statistics.

## Data Security and Backups

Since your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We highly recommend securing your device with a strong passcode or biometric authentication.

You are solely responsible for creating backups of your data. The App may provide manual export/import functionality so you can back up your records to a secure location of your choosing.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we ever change the functionality of the App in the future to include cloud backups, analytics, or any feature that transmits data off your device, we will update this Privacy Policy and explicitly request your consent before doing so.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
